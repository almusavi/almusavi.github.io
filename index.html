Swingers

The greatest 90's Movie of All Time

Swingers is movie made in 1996 about struggling actors trying to make it in Hollywood. It launched the Hollywood careers of Vince Vaughn and Jon Favreau. If was written by Jon and directed by Doug Liman.

Actor Character Description
Jon Favreau Mike Peters A down on his luck writer/comedian who just broke up with his girlfriend and forever thinks it was the wrong decision.
Vince Vaughn Trent Walker Mikes' good friend. Trent is the leader of the group. He has all the luck with the ladies. He has all the best lines.
Ron Livingston Rob Ron is Mike's friend who also came up from New York. He is also a struggling actor. He works as 'Goofy' at Disneyland.
Patrick Van Horn Sue Named after the Johnny Cash Song "A Boy Named Sue". Carries a gun.
Alex Desert Charles Another buddy. Bar Hops with the crew.
Heather Graham Lorraine Mike's love interest in the movie.
